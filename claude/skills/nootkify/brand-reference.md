# Nootka Saunas — Brand Reference (bundled)

Portable copy of the Nootka brand rules for the `nootkify` skill, condensed
from the Nootka Guidebook v2022.1. If a `DESIGN_GUIDELINES.md` exists in the
working directory, that file is authoritative and wins on any conflict.

## Brand essence

Cedar barrel saunas, "Made in British Columbia." Rooted in the Pacific coast:
fog, cedar, water, fire. **Soul: adventure, peace, and discovery.**

**Voice & tone** — calm, grounded, premium but unpretentious; nature-forward
and place-specific (PNW, BC, coast, cedar); wellness-oriented. Short, confident,
sensory, declarative statements. No hype, no jargon, no exclamation marks.

Model microcopy:
- "Bring the Spa Experience Home"
- "From the forested shores of the Pacific"
- "Made in BC — Spa quality for your backyard"
- "Built in British Columbia"

## Color

Two greens, two blues, one bright orange. Greens and dark blue dominate; orange
is a sparing accent. Light steel-blue/grey is the neutral background.

| Name | Hex | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Steam | `#CDD8DA` | light neutral — backgrounds, surfaces, cards |
| Coastal Water | `#073D44` | dark blue-teal — primary dark / text on light |
| Reindeer Moss | `#919A3C` | olive — secondary, supporting blocks/tags |
| Evergreen | `#324126` | deep forest — primary brand dark / sections |
| Heat | `#FF821B` | orange — accent only (CTAs, logo flame, highlights) |

Rules:
- Dark sections: Evergreen or Coastal Water with white/Steam text.
- Light sections: Steam or white with Evergreen/Coastal Water text.
- Heat is an accent, **not** a surface — logo flame, primary buttons, links,
  focus states, small highlights. Never flood large areas with orange.
- Reindeer Moss for supporting blocks, tags, secondary panels.
- WCAG AA: never Heat text on Steam; check Steam on Reindeer Moss.

## Typography

**DM Sans only** — the single font family for headings, body, and UI.
- Bold (700): headlines, display, emphasis.
- Regular (400): body, labels, captions. (500 available for medium UI.)
- Headlines: tight-to-normal tracking, generous size jump over body.
- Body: comfortable line-height (~1.4–1.6).
- Sentence case; human capitalization, not all-caps.
- The PDF's monospace running heads are document chrome — do **not** add a
  second font to product UI.

## Logo

Icon (cedar/flame mark) + "nootka saunas" wordmark. **Approved versions only:**
1. Stacked — icon above wordmark. 2. Horizontal — icon left of wordmark.

- Do not recreate, redraw, rotate, recolor outside palette, or rearrange.
- Clear space = width of the "O" in the wordmark (icon alone = width of icon).
  No type or partner logos inside the clear space.
- Co-branding: separate from other logos with a vertical divider; respect
  clearance both sides; never let logos touch.
- Provide the logo as an SVG asset; do not typeset the wordmark in DM Sans as a
  substitute. Approved colorways aren't defined yet — do not invent them; ask
  for approved assets. If none provided, use a clearly marked placeholder.

## Imagery & photography

Natural, cinematic, Pacific Northwest. Categories: product/exterior (cedar
barrel saunas in forest/shore/snow), interior (warm cedar, soft natural light),
in-the-field/lifestyle (real people, après-ski/backyard/coastline; adventure +
calm), texture/detail (stacked firewood, fog, fire, coastline).

- Favor natural light, muted earthy tones, real environments. Avoid studio
  looks, heavy filters, staged stock.
- Cedar tones, greens, fog-greys, coastal blues — consistent with palette.
- People look authentic and active, not posed.
- Full-bleed imagery pairs with dark green/teal blocks in split layouts.

## Layout patterns

- **Split layouts:** solid brand-color panel beside full-bleed photo/content.
- **Generous whitespace** with strong type hierarchy; let headlines breathe.
- **Rounded/arched framing:** the arch and circular form echoes the barrel, but
  keep it a subtle, deliberate accent — reserve it for a select hero element
  (e.g. a single circular image mask or one arched section top). Default to
  tighter radii and flatter UI; do not apply roundness pervasively.
- Keep UI clean and minimal; product and photography carry the page.

## Do / Don't

**Do** — DM Sans (Bold + Regular) everywhere; exact palette hexes with Heat as
sparing accent; only stacked/horizontal logo with clear space; natural
place-specific imagery and calm confident copy.

**Don't** — introduce other fonts; recolor/redraw/rotate/rearrange the logo;
fill large areas with orange or use it as a background; crowd the logo; use
staged, heavily filtered, or off-brand stock photography.
